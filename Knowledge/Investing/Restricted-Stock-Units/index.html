    • Ownership of RSUs is transferred from your employer to you based on a vesting plan. Once ‘vested’, those shares are basically normal assets under your ownership â€” you’re free to hold or sell as you please.
    • There are important things to know about how they’re taxed. The main difference is that the entire value of the stocks are considered your income.
