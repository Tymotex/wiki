## Smoothies
- Kale + Pineapple + Banana
- Blueberries + Banana
- Banana + Oatmeal

**Tips**:
- In all smoothies, consider adding:
    - Ground flaxseeds.
    - Chia seeds.
    - Turmeric.
- Freeze fruits instead of crushing ice.
- Use homemade soy milk or oat milk to cut costs.
